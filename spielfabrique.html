Our programs

Founded in 2016, SpielFabrique has developed an international and cross-border expertise of the video game industry.

From mentoring indie game studios looking to bring their first game to market or established indie studios looking to scale up, to its consulting business towards public entities, SpielFabrique has built a comprehensive understanding of the video game ecosystem.

Our Partners

Who are we?

SpielFabrique stems from a Franco-German video game initiative created in April 2015 by Media Deals at the French Embassy on the occasion of the International Games Week Berlin.

Pr. Odile LimpachCo-Founder
Odile teaches at Cologne Game Lab and advises on serious games. She held executive roles at Blue Byte, Ubisoft, and volunteers in vocational training and creative industries development.

odile@spielfabrique.eu

Thierry BaujardCo-Founder
Thierry excels in funding creative industries, directing companies in Audiovisual, Gaming, MusicTech, FashionTech, and is the founder of the pan-European network, Media Deals.

thierry@spielfabrique.eu

Matthes LindnerEVA / NEXUS Project Manager
Matthes leads Spielfabriques EVA Program, guiding studios toward success and growth. With a background in anthropology, sustainability, and project management, he's passionate about democratizing video game culture through the sustainability NEXUS.

matthes@spielfabrique.eu

Olympe ChallotMATCH / BRIDGE Project Manager
Olympe supports OFAJ/DFJW's AbP program. She holds a Masters degree in Intl Relations, specialized in Creative Europe's MEDIA sub-program and worked as Communication & Advocacy Officer in an NGO before joining her current team.

olympe@spielfabrique.eu

Emma DauphinCross-sectorial Project Manager
Emma comes from the Sustainability and Agriculture research field but has over the years worked closely with multiple creative industries. She is in charge of various Cross-Media projects and part of the communication team.

emma@spielfabrique.eu

Joris SchnabelSenior Operations Manager
Joris is a producer and project manager with a background in event production, creative operations, and public funding in the games industry. Before joining Spielfabrique, he co-founded the Berlin-based indie studio Dexai Arts, served as Head of Exhibition at the Games Ground Festival, and helped shape industry events with Beat Em Hub. As Senior Operations Manager, he oversees the implementation of key projects and supports the coordination of partners and production workflows.

joris@spielfabrique.eu

Fernanda PaganiProject Manager
Fernanda is Project Manager at SpielFabrique, supporting their cross-media projects like LMPG, KinoGames, and the Book Fair, as well as community building. She co-founded Beat Em Hub and Games Ground Berlin, a government-funded gaming industry festival and conference where she manages the B2B program and curates speakers and mentors.

fernanda@spielfabrique.eu

Jrmy LeresteuxIT / Communication Manager
Jrmy manages SpielFabrique's community and IT. With a double background in science and humanities, he teaches french and develops websites and applications.

jeremy@spielfabrique.eu
